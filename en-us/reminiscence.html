Reminiscence

This project was made for the Global Game Jam 2023. The theme was ROOTS.
Me and my group decided to represent the last moments of a gamer remembering the games that marked his lifetime. Walking by the rooms, the player can interact with old classic arcade games known as the roots of the game industry.
As the games are being remembered by the character, we decided to not use online assets for the arcade games, neither look at the original game rules during the development, assuming some of our memories might not reflect exactly how the game really was.

Check my Global Game Jam game page to download and test this project.

    Instructions:

  • Use WASD buttons to move
  • Use E button to interact
