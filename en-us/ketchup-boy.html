Ketchup Boy

Collect all the coins to open the passage to the next level.
Avoid the enemies, mustard man and the pipe fishes, or kill it using the ketchup blast.
Avoid the map hazards candles, relish pool and water.

  • Use keyboard arrows ( ) or WASD buttons to move
  • Use Control/Mouse Click button to shoot ketchup blast

All 2D assets used are from Kenney Assets for study purpose. Some were modified by myself to fit the project.
